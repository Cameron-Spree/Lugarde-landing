Mastery in Every Joint.

Comparing standard garden construction with the Lugarde Premier engineering philosophy. The difference is in the details you can't see.

01

Prima 3-in-1 System

Our patented Prima system uses milled aluminum strips inside the timber joints, creating a completely wind and water-tight seal without the need for visible nails or screws. This allows for natural wood movement without compromising structural seal.

Seal Rating Class 4 Air Tight
Assembly Mechanical Lock
02

Northern Sourced Pine

Standard buildings use fast-grown timber. Lugarde exclusively uses pine from northern regions where cold climates force trees to grow slowly, resulting in tighter growth rings and a naturally denser, more durable material.

Fiber Density +45% vs Standard
Moisture Content 14% Kiln Dried
